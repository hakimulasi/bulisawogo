Tyler Farr Biography and Wiki

Tyler Farr is an American country music singer and songwriter born and raised in Garden City, Missouri. Farr is famously known for a song like “A Guy Walks into a Bar”, “Redneck Crazy” and “Whiskey in my Water”. While signed to o BNA Records, Farr released two singles for the label before it closed.Farr later transferred to Columbia Records Nashville where he released two albums: Redneck Crazy in 2013 and Suffer in Peace in 2015. Nonetheless, Farr has charted eight singles on the Billboard Hot Country Songs and Country Airplay chart. Farr’s highest ranking on the latter is “A Guy Walks Into a Bar”. “A Guy Walks Into a Bar placed at No. 1 in 2015.

Tyler Farr Career

Farr co-wrote the songs “Hey Y’all” for Cole Swindell. He also released his debut single, “Hot Mess in early 2012. Farr co-wrote “Hot Mess” with Rhett Akins. On September 30, 2013, he released his debut album, Redneck Crazy. He also led off his second album in mid-2014 with “A Guy Walks Into a Bar”.

On April 28, 2015, he released an album, entitled Suffer in Peace.”A Guy Walks Into a Bar” became Farr’s first No. 1 single on Billboard Country Airplay in May 2015. In late 2016, he released his eighth single which was co-written by Seth Ennis. Farr parted ways with Sony Music Nashville in 2018 and signed with Broken Bow Records in 2019.
