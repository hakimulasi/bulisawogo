What is Aldo Gallardo's Net Worth?

Aldo Gallardo is a Mexican actor who has a net worth of $3 million. Aldo Gallardo was born in Tijuana, Baja, California. Gallardo studied acting in Televisa's Center for Arts Education. He appeared in the TV series Al diablo con los guapos starring as Fernando from 2007 to 2008. In 2009 Aldo appeared in the television series Sexo y otros secretos. From 2008 to 2009 he starred as Hector in the TV series Un gancho al corazon.

He starred as Manuel Solis in the TV series Mi pecado in 2009 as well as in the short Quiero saber la verdad. In 2010 Gallardo appeared in the television series Morir en Martes and Morir en Martes 2 in 2011. He also appeared in El Octavo Mandamiento in 2011. Gallardo appeared in the TV series Infames in 2012 as well as La Patrona and Las Trampas del Deseo in 2013. In 2014 he starred as Carlos "Charlie" Delgado in the TV series Dos Lunas and also appeared in the series Los Miserables. Gallardo also worked as an assistant production manager and location manager for the 2009 short Quiero saber la verdad.
