The 'Yellowjackets' Cast at the 75th Primetime Emmy Awards

Publish date: 2024-07-03

We might not know if supernatural forces are toying with Yellowjackets' stranded soccer stars in the Canadian wilderness, but we do know that the cast showed up and showed out on the 75th Primetime Emmy Awards red carpet. While some of the Yellowjackets cast like Juliette Lewis in red-hot Moschino leaned into their characters' personalities, others like Sophie Thatcher went in the opposite direction. Then you've got Jasmine Savoy Brown and Liv Hewson, who both delivered maximum glam with seemingly less regard for their onscreen counterparts.

Regardless of whether or not they stayed on theme, suffice it to say, these state champs and wilderness survivors ate up the red carpet. Read on to see what all the buzz is about.

Jasmine Savoy Brown in Givenchy

Jasmine Savoy Brown's sheer Givenchy dress was as ethereal as the presence that may or may not be stalking the Wiskayok High School state soccer champs in the wilderness. As for her rhinestone-adorned Jimmy Choos? They were among the best shoes of the night.

Sophie Thatcher

Teenage Natalie might now have approved of Sophie Thatcher's elegant and on (color) trend gown, but we certainly do.

Juliette Lewis in Moschino

Juliette Lewis AKA present-day Natalie looked red hot in a sequin Moschino gown and Hanut Singh jewelry.

Liv Hewson

We know Van would be head-over-heels for the clean lines and sharp edges in Liv Hewson's Emmys suit. Equally alluring and dashing, the vibe is black-tie gender-bending goodness.

Courtney Eaton in Jason Wu

All hail the (Season 1) antler queen! Courtney Eaton looked resplendent in a sheer and lacy Jason Wu gown that both witchy teen Lottie and grownup cult leader Lottie would approve of.

Sophie Nelisse

Since adult Shana spent her life paying for the sins of her past, we honestly don't know if she'd like Sophie Nelisse's sheer draped gown, but we bet Jackie would.

Samantha Hanratty in Dolce & Gabbana

Samantha Hanratty's romantic Dolce & Gabbana gown is exactly as sweet and innocent as the front everyone's favorite psychopath, Misty Quigley, likes to present to the world. We're charmed, but we think Christina Ricci's look ahead is a more honest portrait of our beloved curly-haired cannibal.

Tawny Cypress

As adult Taissa, Tawny Cypress has to channel not one, but two distinct personas: that of a forthright put-together politician and the decidedly not put-together other one. Her 2024 Emmys gown was a delicious blend of the two: impeccably tailored and dazzling, but also darkly vampy.

Christina Ricci in Saint Laurent

Christina Ricci's sleek Saint Laurent gown and snaky Martin Katz jewelry do a much better job of revealing the essence of the Yellowjackets character she shares with Samantha Hanratty.
