Mike Myers' Wife Kelly Tisdale Gives Birth Baby Girl Sunday Molly

Publish date: 2024-08-04

Looks like the weekend came early for Mike Myers! The Austin Powers actor, 50, and his wife, Kelly Tisdale, welcomed their second child on Friday, April 11 — a baby daughter named Sunday Molly, his rep confirms to Us Weekly.

"We couldn't be happier," the proud second-time parents tell Us.

Together since 2006, Myers and Tisdale secretly married in New York City in 2010. They had their first child, son Spike, now 2, in September 2011; last October, they revealed they were expecting their second.
