Where does Mike Weir live? What nationality is Mike Weir?

Publish date: 2024-08-26

Mike Weir is a Canadian professional golfer who is left-handed and plies his trade on the PGA Tour. Mike Weir is a three-time winner of the Lionel Conacher Award and was a one-time winner of the Lou Marsh Trophy.

Where does Mike Weir live?

Mike Weir lives in Sandy, Utah, in Canada.

What nationality is Mike Weir?

Mike Weir is a Canadian.

Mike Weir commenced his golf career at Saint Michael Elementary School then proceeded to Saint Clair Secondary School, and then played collegiate golf at Brigham Young University.

Mike Weir commenced his professional career in 1992 and his best ranking was when he placed 3rd in Official World Golf Ranking in June 2003.

Mike Weir is the only Canadian to ever win a Masters Tournament and he was inducted into the Canadian Sports Hall of Fame. Mike Weir was a one-time Ontario Junior Championship winner and a two-time Ontario Amateur Championship winner.
