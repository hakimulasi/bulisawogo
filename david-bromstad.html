David Bromstad is an American designer and television personality.

David is the son of Diane Marlys (Krueger) and Richard Harold David Bromstad. David’s siblings are Dean, Dynelle, and Dyonne.

David’s father has Norwegian ancestry. David’s mother is of German and Swedish descent.

David’s paternal grandfather was Joseph M. Bromstad (the son of Lars Jacobsen Bromstad and Ingeborg Anna). Joseph was born in Minnesota, to parents from Norway.

David’s paternal grandmother was Mildred Irene Espeseth (the daughter of Peder K. Espeseth and Gina Emilia Rud/Rude). Peder was the son of Norwegian parents. Gina was the daughter of Jorgen O. Rud/Rude, who was Norwegian, and of Mari, whose parents were Norwegian.

David’s maternal grandfather was Erwin/Ervin Albert Krueger (the son of John A. F. Krueger and Fredrika Emilia/Emilie Wolf). John was born in Wisconsin, to parents from Germany. Fredrika was also born in Wisconsin, to parents from Germany.

David’s maternal grandmother was Doris Anne Leff (the daughter of Emil Leonard Leff and Selma Kristina Eriksson). Emil was the son of Erick Leaf, who was from Sweden, and of Caroline Ericson, who was also Swedish. Selma was Swedish herself.
