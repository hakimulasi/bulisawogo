Stray Kids is a South Korean boy band created in 2017 by JYP Entertainment. The group’s members were picked as a result of the 2017 reality show Stray Kids. Their pre-debut EP, Mixtape, was published in January 2018.

Their formal debut, however, came on March 25, 2018, with the release of their debut EP I Am Not. The EP debuted at number four on Gaon’s Album Chart, selling over 54,000 copies in March alone. Stray Kids members made their debut international appearance in April 2018. Their third and fourth EPs, named I Am Who and I Am You, respectively, were released in August and October of the same year.

Meet the Stray Kids Members

Bang Chan, Woojin, Lee Know, Changbin, Hyunjin, Han, Felix, Seungmin, and I.N are the members of the boy band Stray Kids.

Bang Chan

Birthday: October 3, 1997

Christopher Bang, better known as Bang Chan, was born on October 3, 1997. Stray Kids’ frontman, lead vocalist, lead dancer, and producer. Although his birthplace is unknown, he moved to Australia when he was a child. He joined the group in 2010 after attending a JYP tryout in Australia.

Changbin
Birthdate: August 11, 1999

Seo Chang-bin, also known as Changbin, was born in Yongin, South Korea on August 11, 1999. He is the main rapper, vocalist, and producer for the Stray Kids boy band. Changbin is a naturally quick talker, which is how he became interested in rapping. His idols are BIGBANG’s G-dragon and Kendrick Lamar. He says if he wasn’t in Stray Kids, he’d be a producer, writer, or tattoo artist.

He speaks English fluently and enjoys dancing, listening to music, traveling, beatboxing, and shopping for clothes.
