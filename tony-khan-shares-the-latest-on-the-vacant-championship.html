Tony Khan Shares the Latest on the Vacant Championship

Publish date: 2024-08-16

Ladies and gentlemen, the wrestling world is buzzing with excitement as Tony Khan, the man behind the AEW juggernaut, drops some major news about the vacant Ring of Honor Television Championship!

In a move that emphasizes the rich history of this prestigious title, Khan has announced that the best stage to crown a new champion is none other than the upcoming Ring of Honor Honor Club exclusive pay-per-view event, Final Battle, set to go down in Garland, Texas.

So, what’s the game plan? Well, on December 15, at Final Battle, we’re in for a showdown of epic proportions with a six-way ‘Survival of the Fittest’ match to determine the next ROH Television Champion. This electrifying battle will see six top-notch competitors vying for glory.

But wait, there’s more! In the lead-up to this grand event, we’re going to witness a series of qualifying matches that will determine the lucky wrestlers who will earn their spot among the six participants in this high-stakes showdown.

Tony Khan is already hyping up the “high stakes exciting matches” that will serve as qualifiers, promising fans some heart-pounding action. And let’s not forget the weight of the situation – whoever emerges victorious will have some “big shoes” to fill, given the prestigious history of the ROH Television Championship.

For those who missed it, Samoa Joe made a noble decision to vacate the ROH World TV Championship on a recent episode of AEW Dynamite. His aim? To shift his focus toward the pursuit of the AEW World Heavyweight Champion, MJF. It’s a move that signals a new era for the Samoan Submission Machine.

So, wrestling fans, get ready for Final Battle on December 15, where the next ROH Television Champion will be crowned in a fiery showdown of survival. It’s a date you won’t want to miss!
