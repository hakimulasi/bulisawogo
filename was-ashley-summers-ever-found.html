Was Ashley Summers ever found?

Publish date: 2024-08-14

Ashley Summers was last seen at around 6:00 p.m. on July 4, 2007. She was last seen in the area of West 96th Street and Madison Avenue. Earlier that day, Summers went to a pool party at which she was the only guest.

After spending a few hours swimming in the pool, she decided to visit her aunt Christina, who lived within a 10-minute walking distance away from the pool but never arrived. Because she was frequently staying with her relatives, her disappearance went unnoticed until two days later.
