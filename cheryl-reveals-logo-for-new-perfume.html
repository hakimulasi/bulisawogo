Cheryl reveals logo for new perfume

Publish date: 2024-08-11

‘Crazy Stupid Love’ singer Cheryl Fernandez-Versini, revealed the new logo for her third fragrance on Sunday.

The Geordie star also announced the name of her latest scent on the same day.

The X Factor judge has revealed that her new perfume will be called StormFlower Noir, which means StormFlower Black in French.

After the success of her first two record breaking StormFlower’s, Cheryl has taken a different route with the newest perfume and has moved away from the light florally look of the first two.

Unfortunately it has been confirmed by SAS And Company PR, that there will not be a new commercial for StormFlower Noir.

Cheryl’s third fragrance in the StormFlower range will launch on August 18th.
