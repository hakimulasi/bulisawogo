Who is Kyle Bugha?

Kyle Bugha aka Bugha is an American e-sports player. He is popular for his amazing gaming skills in playing Fortnite.

For his talents, he won Fortnite World Cup and The Game Awards in 2019. He was also nominated for Best in Gaming (Shorty Award) in 2020.
