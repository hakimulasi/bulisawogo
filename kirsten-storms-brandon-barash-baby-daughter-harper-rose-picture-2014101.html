Kirsten Storms, Brandon Barash Welcome Baby Girl Harper Rose: Picture

Publish date: 2024-08-24

Welcome to the world, little girl! General Hospital actress Kirsten Storms gave birth to her first child with husband Brandon Barash, daughter Harper Rose, on Tuesday, Jan. 7, the couple announced via Twitter.

"Our passenger has touched down and arrived," 34-year-old Barash tweeted. "Welcome to our world. There has not been a more fulfilling day." He then posted a photo of his newborn's hospital chart, which revealed her name, Harper. 

Two days later, on Thursday, Jan. 9, the proud papa took to Twitter again to share a photo of the little bundle of joy with her mom, 29. "Just the three of us…well, the Queen, the Princess, and a dad's hand," he captioned the black-and-white shot. "Harper Rose, you've already left an indelible footprint on my soul."

Former Days of Our Lives star Storms tweeted a picture of the baby, too. "Daddy's lil princess," she wrote alongside the Instagram snap of her 2-day-old daughter.
