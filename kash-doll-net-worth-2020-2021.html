Career

Kash Doll started her career performing in local clubs and at schools and doing charity events in the Detroit area. At the same time, she signed a contract with a local record company, which later fell through.

On her 26th birthday, she released the mixtape, Brat Mail, dedicated to her fans and followers. She also signed a deal with the Republic Records and released the first single with the label “Ice Me Out.

Doll released the song “Hustla” in 2019 in which she described her experience working at strip clubs.
