Who Is Brenda Lee? - Brenda Lee Wikipedia

Brenda Lee Wikipedia: - Brenda Lee was born on December 11, 1944, in Atlanta, Georgia, US. Brenda Lee is an American singer. She had her first Billboard success at the age of 12 in 1957, playing rockabilly, pop, country, and Christmas songs, and was dubbed "Little Miss Dynamite." "Sweet Nothin's", "I'm Sorry", "I Want to Be Wanted", "Speak to Me Pretty", "All Alone Am I" and "Losing You" are among Lee's most hit tracks. Her 1958 holiday tune "Rockin' Around the Christmas Tree" topped the US Billboard Hot 100 in 2023, becoming Lee the oldest artist to do so. 

Lee went to elementary school wherever her father worked, which was mostly between Atlanta and Augusta. Her family was impoverished. She shared a bed with her brother and sister as a youngster in a series of three-room dwellings without running water. Her life revolved on her parents' employment, their family, and the Baptist church, where she began singing solos every Sunday.
