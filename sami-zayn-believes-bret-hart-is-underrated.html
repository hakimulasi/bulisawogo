Sami Zayn Believes Bret Hart Is Underrated

Publish date: 2024-08-25

Sami Zayn may be one of the best heels in pro wrestling on WWE television right now but on social media, he’s making sure that everyone knows how he really feels.

Someone who everyone knows to be one of the best of all time is none other than Bret Hart, a man who has been an important figure to Sami during his career.

After all, The Hitman was the one who introduced Zayn prior to his huge match against John Cena that gave him his start on the main roster.

The idea that anyone can underrate someone like Bret Hart just tells you how good he really was.

It’s a shame his career ended in the manner that it did, but in a fantasy booking world, Zayn would’ve had the opportunity to go head to head with Bret in a Battle of Canada type of match.

Now, though, we should all remember him as the legend he was in the ring.
