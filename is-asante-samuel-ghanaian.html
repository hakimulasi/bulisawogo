Is Asante Samuel Ghanaian?

Publish date: 2024-07-03

Former professional quarterback for the Atlanta Falcons, Asante Samuel is a Ghanaian-born national.

Where in Ghana was Asante Samuel born?

Asante Samuel was born in Accra, Ghana.

Was Asante Samuel raised in Ghana?

Asante Samuel was born in Ghana but he was raised in Fort Lauderdale, Florida.

Asante Samuel education

What is Asante Samuel‘s educational background? Which schools did Asante Samuel attend? Where did Asante Samuel attend school? Asante Samuel after moving to the United States at a very young age, attended Boyd H. Anderson High School in  Lauderdale Lakes, Florida, where he grew up. He continued his education at the University of Central Florida right after high school.

Asante Samuel high school career

Did Asante Samuel play football in high school? Was Asante Samuel a good high school football player? Asante Samuel played very well in high school. He threw 1,800 yards and rushed for 500 yards as a junior quarterback. He even earned an All-State honors in high school.
