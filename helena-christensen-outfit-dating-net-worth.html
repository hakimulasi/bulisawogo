Helena Christensen Looks Elegant In Her Outfit

Publish date: 2024-07-07

The Danish supermodel, Helena Christensen, has still got her beauty despite her growing age. Christensen, who is now 51, revealed that she rediscovered a bodysuit from her early modeling days, including a portrait of her face.

The supermodel looked elegant and beautiful with the outfit she once wore on the runway and posed for the picture on 7 September 2020. She donned in a full glam makeup with a bold red lip and styled the look with rocker heels with no pants.

Posting the picture on her Instagram, Christensen wrote, 'One of my favorite designers ever, Christian Lacroix gave me this bodysuit with my portrait on it after I wore it in one of his shows in my early twenties and I just found it again.'

Who Is Helena Christensen Dating Now?

Helena Christensen is single now, with no traces of her partner. But she was in one of the famous relationships back in a few decades. She previously dated an Australian musician Michael Hutchence from 1991 to 1995. Her former love interest died on 22 November 1997.

Recalling their relationship of four years, Helena Christensen mentioned that the bond was 'joyful, sweet, deep, and emotional.'

She spoke to Marie Claire and said, '[It was] total mental and physical chemistry.'

Christensen cited about Hutchence's 1992 brain injury after the assault from a taxi driver and how he grew 'dark and very angry' after losing his sense of smell. The lovebirds departed in 1995, and after a couple of years, the Australian singer took his own life in 1997.

Later, Christensen started dating The Walking Dead star, Norman Reedus, in 1998. Helena Christensen and Norman Reedus relished their relationship for four years until 2003 and welcomed a song named Mingus, who graduated from high school in 2018.

How Much Is Helena Christensen Net Worth?

Helena Christensen has a net worth of $20 million, which she garners as a fashion model and actress.

Christensen, who is a former Miss Universe Demark winner, is also a former Victoria's Secret Angels and a photographer. Besides, she is a creative director of Nylon magazine.

As an actress, she starred in several movies, including Ready to Wear (1994), Allegro (2004), The Christmas Party (2009), The Reunion 3 (2016), and many others.

Also, she appeared in Chris Isaak's music video Wicked Game, which was ranked 4th on VH1's Sexiest Video Moments and 13th on VH1's "100 Greatest Videos". It got featured on MTV's 'Sexiest Video of All-Time' as well.
