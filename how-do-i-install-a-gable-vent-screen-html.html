How are gable vents measured?

How to Measure Gable Vents

Measure the length and width of your attic floor with a tape measure.

Multiply the length by the width to determine the square footage of attic space you need to ventilate.

Divide your total square footage by 300 to determine the vent area.

Purchase gable vents that match the net vent area from your calculations.
