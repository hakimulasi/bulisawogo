Early Life

Peter Brian Gabriel was born in Chobham village in Surrey, United Kingdom. He was born on February 13, 1950, to his mother Edith Irene Allen, and his father Ralph Parton Gabriel who worked as an electrical engineer but he passed away in 2012.

Peter Gabriel was mostly grown and raised in his hometown in Surrey by his parents. He doesn’t have any siblings that we know of. Peter Gabriel completed his early schooling from a private school named Cable House, followed by the St. Andrew’s Preparatory School and the Charterhouse School.

For his personal life, Peter Gabriel has been into marriages, a divorce, relationships and has children that we know of and that is confirmed. His first marriage was to the daughter of Philip Moore who was Queen Elizabeth II private secretary, Jill Moore. Career

Peter Gabriel first found Genesis band along with his school friends. He has seven studio albums released with the band starting with “From Genesis to Revelation” and ending with “A Trick of the Tail.” Peter Gabriel was responsible for giving the band a unique image with the costumes he developed.

Peter Gabriel left the band in 1975, but he went back to work with them only for the 1982 concert “Six of the Best.” His debut self-titled album “Peter Gabriel” was released in 1977, and it had singles that ranked on top charts. Peter Gabriel’s latest studio album “New Blood” was released in 2011 and is listed on top charts in 4 different countries including his home country.
