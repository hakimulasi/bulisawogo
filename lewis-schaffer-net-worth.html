Full Overview and Wiki

The Journey Begins

It wasn’t long before Lewis Schaffer etched his name into the annals of comedy history with his show Free until Famous. This wasn’t just any show; it was a marathon of laughter that claimed the title of the longest running solo stand-up show in London and perhaps all of Britain, with over 400 performances. His stint at the Leicester Square Theatre with Lewis Schaffer: American in London further solidified his status, becoming the longest solo comedy residency at the theatre.

The Radio Waves Beckon

Schaffer didn’t just limit his talents to the stage. Nunhead American Radio with Lewis Schaffer became a beacon for his wit on Resonance 104.4FM London, live every Monday, attracting guests like Russell Howard, Richard Herring, and Stewart Lee.

This show wasn’t just a platform for laughs; it was a testament to Schaffer’s knack for engaging and entertaining discussions, making the mundane mesmerizing.

Awards and Accolades
