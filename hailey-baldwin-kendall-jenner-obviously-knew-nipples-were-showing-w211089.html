Hailey Baldwin: Kendall Jenner Obviously Knew Nipples Were Showing

Publish date: 2024-07-09

Not a nip slip! Hailey Baldwin told Us Weekly that her BFF Kendall Jenner’s recent headline-making sheer outfit was no wardrobe malfunction.

“She obviously knew the shirt was gonna be see-through, but she owns it,” the 19-year-old model told Us at the Coach High Line party in downtown Manhattan on Wednesday, June 22. “She doesn’t really care about what people have to say.”

Baldwin added that she, Jenner and pal Gigi Hadid were just enjoying a day strolling through the city together on Tuesday, June 21, when Jenner’s eye-catching outfit grabbed the attention of photographers.

“All we do is … we do normal things,” she said. “We go out to eat. We hang out at each other’s houses. We watch movies.”

On Tuesday, the photogenic threesome looked totally ’90s in matching black-and-blue jean getups. (Baldwin rocked a black mini and leather platform ankle boots.)

Hadid, 21, wore a black T-shirt with the sleeves rolled up, flared blue jeans and a black belt. Jenner, 20, wore a nearly identical outfit, except with an off-shoulder sheer black top that revealed her nipple piercing.

“Having my nipple pierced isn’t really something I make a big deal of, but I think people are obsessed with the topic because it seems so unexpected of me,” she wrote on her site, kendallj.com, in 2015. “Before I even decided to go for it, I just like showing my nipples through some of my outfits; there’s something understated yet sexy about it.”
