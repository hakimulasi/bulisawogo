Johnny Lever is an Indian actor, singer, and comedian who is considered as one of the most talented comedians in Bollywood. He has starred in over 300 films and is best remembered for his comedic roles in films like Kuch Kuch Hota Hai (1998), Deewana Mastana (1997), Baazigar (1993), Dulhe Raja (1998), Dilwale (2015), Koi Mil Gaya (2003), Chalte Chalte (2003), Dulhe Raja (1998), Humraaz (2002), Judaai (1997), Ajnabee (2001), Raja Hindustani (1996), Love Ke Liye Kuch Bhi Karega (2001), Phir Bhi Dil Hai Hindustani (2000), Kaho Naa Pyaar Hai (2000), Kunwara (2000), Anari No. 1 (1999), Karan Arjun (1995), etc.

    Johnny Lever Facts

  • He had to quit school after the seventh standard due to financial difficulties.
  • One of Lever’s most memorable live performances was when he impersonated Michael Jackson during the 1999 Filmfare Awards.
  • He acted alongside his daughter in the comedy film Housefull 4 (2019) starring Akshay Kumar, Riteish Deshmukh, Bobby Deol, Kriti Sanon, Rana Daggubati, etc., and the television series Pop Kaun? (2023) alongside Kunal Khemu, Nupur Sanon, Farhad Samji, Saurabh Shukla, Chunky Panday, and Rajpal Yadav.
  • While working at Hindustan Unilever company, he performed at a company function by mimicking a few senior officials and earned the nickname ‘Johnny Lever’. Years later, when he found a foothold in the film industry, he chose to use that as his stage name.
  • He was awarded the Filmfare Award for ‘Best Performance in a Comic Role’ 2 times, once for Deewana Mastana (1997) and again the next year for Dulhe Raja (1998). Additionally, he has garnered 11 more Filmfare Award nominations as of 2023.
  • Lever is credited with popularizing stand-up comedy in India.
  • He celebrates his birthday on the same day as popular celebrities like Sunidhi Chauhan, Suchitra, Mohit Raina, Gautam Rode, Steve Martin, Marcia Gay Harden, Magic Johnson, Halle Berry, Susan Olsen, Catherine Bell, Mila Kunis, and many others.
