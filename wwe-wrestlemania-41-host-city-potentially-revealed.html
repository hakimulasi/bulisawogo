WWE WrestleMania 41 Host City Potentially Revealed

Publish date: 2024-08-22

WWE just wrapped up WrestleMania 39 in Los Angeles, and next year’s WrestleMania 40 event will go down in Philadelphia. Now, it seems that we have a top contender for WrestleMania 41 already.

WrestleMania brings a lot of traffic to its host cities, no matter where they decide to hold the event. This huge influx of outside revenue also brings millions into host cities, and it’s understandable that any city would love to host such a huge event.

According to Patrick Kessler, Special Contributor WCCO-TV/CBS Minneapolis, his city is in the running to get WrestleMania in 2025. This would be an epic deal for Minneapolis.

“Sources tell me Minneapolis is a finalist city for WrestleMania 2025.

“This would be a HUGE get.”

Minneapolis is a city that has a very rich history in pro wrestling. That territory was booming years ago, but those days are in the past now. Hopefully, WWE’s possible return to the market sparks another boom for pro wrestling in that region.

WWE has not officially nailed down WrestleMania 41, but they did announce the locations for WrestleMania 39 and 40 at the same time last year. Only time will tell if they bring a huge announcement for Minneapolis fans very soon, but there is already buzz surrounding 2025’s showcase of the immortals.
