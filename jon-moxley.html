Career

Jon first appeared in the ring as “Jon Moxley” in June 2004. Then he joined forces with Jimmy Turner. Meanwhile, they won the HWA Tag Team Championship on May 11, 2005. He also spent six years in HWA. Eventually, in 2006 and 2007, he made three appearances in the WWE (World Wrestling Entertainment), on the TV shows Heat and Velocity. In addition, in 2007, he enrolled in Insanity Pro Wrestling and spent four years there. He also won the IPW World Heavyweight Championships several times.

He competed in the CZW’s Tournament of Death VIII on June 6, 2009. He also took part in several CZW events and worked for more than two years. He later worked for Dragon Gate USA. Similarly, in April 2011, he signed a contract with the WWE as Dean Ambrose. He was on the roster alongside Roman Reigns, Seth Rollins, and The Shield in November 2012. Then he received numerous honors. He also became the WWE’s 27th Triple Crown Champion and 16th Grand Slam Champion.

As a result, he left WWE and began working for the NJPW. In addition, he won the IWGP United States Championship in his debut performance. He became the first wrestler to hold the WWE and IWGP United States Championships at the same time.

Jon Moxley Achievements

Jon has a long list of accomplishments in his field. He first became WWE United States Champion, then WWE World Heavyweight Champion. He won the Money in the Bank Contract in 2016. He also won the IWGP United States Championship and the WWE Intercontinental Championship.
