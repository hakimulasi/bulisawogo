Kourtney Kardashian Regrets Leaving Boyfriend Scott Disick

Publish date: 2024-07-13

Kourtney Kardashian has hope for a change.

Though she dumped longtime love Scott Disick on July 4 weekend when photos surfaced of him groping ex-girlfriend Chloe Bartoli, Kardashian is regretting her actions, a source reveals in the new issue of Us Weekly. "She wants Scott back."

Specifically, a sober Disick, the source says, and Kardashian, 36, is playing hardball by seeking sole custody of their kids Mason, 5, Penelope, 3, and Reign, 7 months. "She hopes this will scare him enough to get help," the insider says of the professional partier, who's been gallivanting from St. Tropez, France, to Las Vegas in recent weeks. "If he gets to the point where he is really changing she'll let him come home.”
