Did Pierre Palmade Attempt Suicide? Does He Have A Depression? Accident Explored

Pierre Palmade’s accident harmed five people, three of whom were critically injured. The details of the crash were not disclosed.

Did The Car Accident Occur Due To A Cocaine Overdose?

An early inquiry following the collision determined that a cocaine overdose perhaps caused French Actor Pierre Palmade’s vehicle accident.

According to toxicology findings, Palmade was driving under the influence of cocaine. The other two passengers in his automobiles may have left the scene due to the narcotics and the fear of being detained.

Palmade will be prosecuted for manslaughter as soon as his condition improves. According to French legislation, a motorist with drugs in his system faces up to two years in prison.

Palmade is anticipated to face prison since he tested positive for drugs and hurt others. The 54-year-old has already been charged with drug use on multiple occasions.

He was convicted of cocaine possession in 1995 and fined 20,000 francs. Pierre officially acknowledged having a drug problem in 2019, claiming that cocaine had wrecked his life since his early twenties.
