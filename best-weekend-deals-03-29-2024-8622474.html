Nordstrom Rack, Tory Burch, Gap, and Amazon

Publish date: 2024-08-31

Across the internet, our go-to brands are celebrating the transition from March to April — aka the time when we finally start to swap winter fashion for spring staples — with major markdowns. All of which we are, of course, shopping.

Nordstrom Rack just launched its Clear the Rack Sale, offering an extra 25 percent off already-marked down items, while Tory Burch’s Spring Event gives you the chance to save up to 54 percent on its luxury bags, footwear, and more by stacking sales. For affordable spring fashion, I’m shopping Gap’s Spring Sale, where classic styles — including loose jeans and linen dresses — are up to 60 percent off. And in terms of skincare, you kind of can’t beat Amazon’s secret beauty sale, where shopper-favorite products start at just $6.

Shop everything we’re eyeing from each sale, below.

74% Off: Sarto by Franco Sarto Georgie Square-Toe Mule

Nordstrom Rack Clear the Rack Sale

Nordstrom Rack’s Clear the Rack Sale is officially here, and I’m using the double-discount event as an opportunity to stock up on spring shoes for way less. Right now, all of the retailer’s already-discounted items are an extra 25 percent off, which makes these Sarto by Franco Sarto loafer mules 74 percent off. Another great deal is on these 72-percent off slingback heels from Vince, which shoppers rave are “so comfy but so chic.” Per another, these are some of the “most comfortable shoes” they own, noting that they wore them all day in the office and then straight to a date.

Also on double sale are these white platform sneakers one customer said are “comfortable and a little dressier” than similar white sneakers, as well as these timeless, pointed-toe ballet flats that look nearly identical to a pair previously worn by Kate Middleton.

54% Off: Tory Burch Small Kira Quilted Satchel

Tory Burch Spring Event

During Tory Burch’s Spring Event, when you spend more than $200 (which is easy, because pretty much every must-have product on the site fits the bill) you can get 25 percent off, and when you spend $500 or more, you unlock 30 percent off. And yes, this includes sale items, like the brand’s iconic Small Kira Quilted Satchel that’s now 54 percent off thanks to those stacked discounts. According to fans of this Kira style, the bag is “buttery smooth,” “stylish and functional,” and “the perfect size.” Another satchel style I’m obsessed with is the spring-ready Mini Swing bag, which is available in five colors, three of which are included in the sale: green, red, and blue.

If you prefer something larger, the McGraw Bucket Bag offers enough room “to carry everything [you] need” and has a sleek, timeless design that goes with everything, according to shoppers. But for something a little more seasonal, consider grabbing the now-$171 Small Ella Printed Tote whose whimsical, Alice in Wonderland-esque print is perfect for spring and summer.

60% Off: Gap Linen-Blend Mini Dress

Gap Spring Sale

Gap has become my go-to for wardrobe basics in the past year thanks to the timeless styles, high quality, and near-constant sales — one of which is happening right now. During the brand’s Spring Sale, select items are up to 60 percent off, including this Linen-Blend Mini Dress a 55-year-old shopper called “very flattering and not too short.” “It’s very cute and appropriate for work,” wrote another. Another loose but elevated style I’ll be wearing spring through summer is the brand’s Organic Cotton Big Shirt, which boasts nearly 600 perfect ratings. This is great for dressing up, but I personally love an oversized button-down worn casually: unbuttoned, over a tank top, and with a pair of loose jeans.

Speaking of jeans, the brand’s now-$35 Mid Rise '90s Loose Jeans are a must-grab. The relaxed style — made almost entirely from cotton, save 1 percent of elastane for stretch — is said to have “the perfect fit.” “It fits me like a glove [and is] comfortable all around,” raved one fan, while another was happy to find that the style doesn’t “squeeze” but also “isn’t too baggy.” Those jeans paired with this 60-percent off belted shacket might be my perfect spring outfit.

$6 Deal: Burt's Bees Almond and Milk Hand Cream

Amazon Skincare Sale

Amazon is the place to stock up on skincare this weekend, with deals on everything from brightening serums to shopper-loved moisturizers. This hand cream from Burt’s Bees, which shoppers say repairs “dry, cracked hands” after just a few days of use, is on sale for just $6. Essenlis Collagen and Retinol Face Cream, a moisturizer designed to smooth out fine-lines and plump skin, is also on sale. 

For a brighter complexion, grab TruSkin’s shopper-loved Vitamin C Face Serum, which boasts more than 93,500 five-star ratings and is on sale for $22. And if your under-eyes need a little TLC, L'Oreal Paris caffeine-infused Eye Defense Eye Cream is an essential. According to one customer, their “puffiness is all but gone within an hour” of application. Another said that their fine lines and wrinkles became less noticeable after just one week.
