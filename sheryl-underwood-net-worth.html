From Humble Beginnings to Stardom

Sheryl Underwood’s journey from Little Rock, Arkansas, to Hollywood’s limelight is nothing short of inspiring.

Born into a family that faced its fair share of challenges, Sheryl’s resilience and humor have been her steadfast companions. Her move to Atwater, California, marked the beginning of a path that would lead her to national recognition.

A Multifaceted Career

Sheryl’s career took off after she became the first female finalist in the 1989 Miller Lite Comedy Search. Her humor, often centered around human sexuality, race relations, and politics, resonates with a wide audience.

Her roles in films like I Got the Hook-Up and Beauty Shop, and her long-standing presence on The Talk since 2011, underline her versatility and staying power in showbiz.
